  Over the years, I've obtained a lot of 2D animated films on home video.      
  This webpage is a catalogue of reviews I've written to help me organize my collection.    
                 
  Is 2D animation dead?              
  Maybe in Hollywood, where only the most popular and commercial methods can survive.    
  But since the decline of American 2D animated feature films, other countries filled the void,    
  including independent films from Japan, France, Ireland, Spain and beyond.      
  With reviews of hundreds of films (old and new) on this site, we have evidence      
  that 2D animation is still alive and well, and widely available to access,        
  IF you seek it out.              
                 
  This site features reviews of 2D animation in feature films and televised Japanese anime.    
  Living in Canada, almost all subjects on this site were once available on DVD or Bluray in North America.
  As physical home media begins to disappear in favor of online streaming,      
  it might be difficult to find shows when they are removed from a service.      
  With these films, you will always have the option to seek out a lost classic on home video,    
  either new or used, to add to your shelf.
